About

Hello,

Welcome to my personal website.

Technology, and the power of the internet excite me. Technology provides the tools, and the internet empowers learning. Together, they allow us to be creators. I recently completed a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp and I am eager for a new challenge to apply the skills I have learned. The work I have completed has provided me with a foundation of current web technologies. I want to build upon this foundation by contributing to the development of technology innovation that connects the world.

I appreciate clean and concise code by applying the principles in my own style. I am most interested in Object-Oriented design, and tools that support the paradigm such as React and JavaScript. I approach design with the user at the forefront. A user interface should be inviting, intuitive, and enjoyable to use. Code should be written efficiently with reusability and maintainability in mind. Every detail matters to me and aids to justify my decisions.

I enjoy working with others, collaboration has infinite possibilities to create something truly unique. I am humbled to learn from others and I look up to those who provide mentorship with their wisdom. At the same time, I don't hesitate to help others who are less experienced or who are eager to discuss a problem.

If you have a few minutes, please check out some of my work and reach out if you would like to connect.

Matthew
